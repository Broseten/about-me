An interactive installation created for the Brno Bold 2024 conference, combining motion tracking and generative visuals to engage visitors in a dynamic art experience.

For the Brno Bold 2024 conference, I created an interactive installation that combines motion tracking with generative visuals. The installation is designed to engage visitors by responding to their movements in real-time.

The app runs in a browser and requires a camera to track the movement of visitors in front of the installation. As people move, their silhouettes are captured and transformed into vibrant, generative visuals projected onto a large screen.

I worked on this project with Šárka Portešová, who helped with the concept and presented the installation at the conference as I could not attend the event.
